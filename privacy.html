Privacy Policy

My goal with EzPz is to collect as little information about you as possible.

Google Analytics

I use a basic Google Analytics setup to measure visits to the site. This is so I know which content is popular and which games I should write more about. There's no point spending hundreds of hours writing articles that no one is reading!

Google Analytics uses cookies to store anonymous data. I do not enable data collection for Advertising Features which collects additional information. Data is not shared with other tools, such as AdSense. I have enabled IP anonymisation. I do not use the User-ID feature to track you persistently. Data is deleted after 26 months.

More information can be found here.

Disqus

Comments are handled by Disqus. You can find out more here.

Contacting Me

My contact form is powered by Formspree. You can read their privacy policy here. When contacting me, you can optionally include a name and email address. This is only so I can get back in touch with you if required, for example if you have a question about a game. I will never sell your contact details.

If you have any questions or comments, please get in touch at milk@ezpz.gg.
