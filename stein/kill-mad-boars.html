Kill Mad Boars

Written by MilkN2Sugars. Last updated 31 December 2018.

Quest Details

How to start

Talk to Plamen Lambert, stood outside the Luscombe House in the south-east of Farshore.

Prerequisites

Must have completed Collect Filled Box and Motivate Fieldworkers. Ability to kill multiple Lvl 6 Mad Boars.

Description

"In the east from here, on the other side of the road we got two more corn fields. Those were not used last year and now some Mad Boars have settled there. Can you kill 6 of them so that they get scared away and we could use the fields this year?"

Requirements

  • Kill Mad Boars x6

Reward

  • 40 Gold
  • 520 Exp
  • Vindicitive Warboots

Quest Walkthrough

As Plamen said, the Mad Boars are found in the fields to the east. They are highlighted on the map below. Use the in-game map (default key M) if you have trouble finding them.

Make your way over there. A couple of the Mad Boars are shown in the screenshot below, but there are many more in the two fields.

The Boars are pretty standard enimies. They attack using melee, and have no special mechanics. But at Lvl 6, they could be a bit too powerful for some very low Lvl players.

As always, make sure you are using your best equipment, and it's always handy to have a couple of health potions on hand (you can buy these from the trader to the north). And if things go really wrong, you can always just run away.

Kill 6 of the Boars, then return to Plamen to complete the quest.
