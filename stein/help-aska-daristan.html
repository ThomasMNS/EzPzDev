Help Aska Daristan

Written by MilkN2Sugars. Last updated 29 December 2018.

Quest Details

How to start

Talk to Aska Daristan, in the Daristan House in Farshore.

Prerequisites

Must have completed Find Aska Daristan.

Description

"You could bring me some ingredients, so I can start cooking. With my injured foot I am not so fast and can not carry heavy things. The ingredients are in the both storage rooms. One of them is upstairs."

Requirements

  • Potatoes
  • Onions
  • Fresh Meat
  • Eggs

Reward

  • 35 Gold
  • 450 Exp
  • Shoulder Pad's

Quest Walkthrough

As Aska says, you will need to get items from two storage rooms. The first is downstairs, in the bottom right of the house.

From Aska, walk right, through one doorway, then another, then down into the storeroom.

The potatoes and onions can be found here. Take one of each.

Exit the storeroom, then go upstairs.

Walk down the hallway to the right.

The storeroom is found at the bottom of the hall, and contains eggs and meat . Again, take one of each.

Return to Aska to complete the quest.
