Kill Wasp Fighter

Written by MilkN2Sugars. Last updated 05 January 2019.

Quest Details

How to start

Talk to Hjalmar Karlsen at the Rusty Bucket Winery in Farshore, stood in the wheat field north-west of the Karlsen House.

Prerequisites

Must be lvl 3.

Description

"Can you kill some of those wasps, so that it is safe to go back home later? You look strong enough to handle this. Not like me. I am already to[sic] weak for something like this."

Requirements

  • Kill Wasp Fighter x6

Reward

  • 20 gold
  • 350 exp
  • Grasscutter (7-13 damage, cost: 6 enegy, 0.1 life regen, 0.75 casttime, 1.5 sec cooldown, lvl 4, 17 gold)

Quest Walkthrough

The Wasp Fighters can be found east of Hjalmar.

Walk right from Haljar over to them.

A couple are highlighted below, but there are many more.

The wasps are fairly standard monsters, with a melee attack that hits the tile directly in front of them. One special feature however is that they inflict poison, which will hit for 1 damage a few times over a couple of seconds.

Simply kill the wasps, and return to Hjalmar to complete the quest.
