Aground: How to catch a Wyrm

Written by MilkN2Sugars. Last updated 28 January 2019.

This short guide will go over how to capture a Wyrm in Aground. You'll need to do this to complete some quests, such as Wyrm Pen, but it isn't immediately obvious how to do so.

Step 1 - Get a net

You need to use a net to catch a Wyrm. You will need at least one net per Wyrm, but since there is a chance the Wyrm can escape (losing your net in the process), you will want to bring along a couple of spares.

To get a net, you will need access to either the Market (available after completing Build Market) or the Dock (available after completing Build Dock).

Using the Market is by far the easiest option. Nets can be bought from the Weapon Dealer for 40 coins each. If you need some cash, you can sell some items to the merchants, for example coal can be sold for 7 coins each.

Alternatively, you can make them yourself. To do this you will need access to cotton seeds. If you don't have these yet, try entering the farm. The Farmer will mention that he has obtained some cotton seeds. If he doesn't, you may need to progress his quest line a bit further (up until about Build Pig Pen).

To start growing cotton, enter the farm management interface, click your field at the top right, select change crop, then select Cotton Seeds.

It will be a couple of minutes before you can harvest it. Each net requires 2 cloth, and each cloth requires 4 cotton, so it takes eight pieces of cotton to craft a net.

With your cotton grown, head over to the Dockhouse, craft your cloth, then craft the net.

Step 2 - Catch the Wyrm

Now, the fun part. To catch a Wyrm, you first need to get it from 8 health, to 6 or below. Very weak weapons, such as a Stone Axe, will not damage a Wyrm. On the other hand, very powerful ones, such as an Earth Hammer, can kill them in one hit so you won't have a chance to catch them. Something like a Steel Axe or Iron Sword works well. To avoid taking damage, you can hit the Wrym from behind, then when it turns around quickly move away then move behind it and repeat. Alternatively try using a Bow and Arrow. You can run faster than the Wyrms so just fire away, then run to a safe distance when the Wyrm gets too close and start firing again.

When the Wyrm is 6 health or below, you will need to lure it into a net. Run ahead of it in the direction it is moving, then place the net by opening your inventory, clicking it and selecting Place. After the Wyrm has run into the net, it will (hopefully) struggle for a few seconds before being captured. However there is a small chance it will escape and you will have to try again.

And that's how you catch a Wyrm in Aground! If you still need help, feel free to ask me in the comments, or check out my many other Aground Guides.
